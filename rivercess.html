Brief Detail:


Rivercess County is a county in the south-central
portion of the West African nation of Liberia. One of
15 counties that compose the first-level of administrative
division in the nation, it has six districts. Cesstos City
serves as the capital with the area of the county measuring
5,594 square kilometres (2,160 sq mi). As of the 2008 Census,
it had a population of 71,509, making it the second least
populous county in Liberia after Grand Kru County.
